# 🚗 Insurance Policy & Claims Analytics Dashboard

A Business Intelligence internship project focused on **simulating insurance policy sales and claims data** to analyze premium collection, claim trends, and risk exposure using **Python and Power BI**.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world **insurance analytics scenario** where policy sales and claim activities are analyzed to understand **profitability, claim patterns, and portfolio risk**.

The dataset represents:

* **1,000,000 insurance policies sold in 2024**
* Claims activity occurring in **2025 and early 2026**
* Different **policy tenures (1–4 years)** to analyze claim exposure.

The final output includes a **Power BI dashboard** that provides an interactive overview of the insurance portfolio.

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### Premium vs Claims

Total premium collected is significantly higher than total claims paid, indicating overall portfolio profitability.

### Policy Tenure Distribution

The majority of policies fall under the **3-year tenure category (~40%)**, showing customer preference for medium-term insurance coverage.

### Claims Trend

Claims appear concentrated in specific months based on the simulated defect conditions.

### Claim Ratio by Tenure

Longer tenure policies tend to have **higher claim exposure**, which highlights increased long-term risk.

### Policy Sales Pattern

Policy purchases remain relatively stable across the year, suggesting consistent demand for insurance policies.
